Income Tax Self Declaration

Investment declaration is basically a list of the tax saving investments which the employee proposes to make during the year. In the absence of such a declaration, the employer will have to start deducting tax at source on the employee’s full estimated salary income without allowing for tax saving deductions. To look upon these tax saving investments is a lot of work to be done. Apoyo provides you with services that will help you in saving the tax.

There are a number of investments through which one can claim tax exemptions. For that the employer needs to have proper knowledge about the rules regarding the same. Apoyo takes care of such legal formalities with the help of its best employees who have proper understanding of such activities. Our team keeps an update on the latest tax benefit schemes that can be provided to our clients. At present there are various ways through which one can claim tax exemption such as Investments in Equity linked saving schemes, PPF, Mutual funds, Housing loan repayment, Mediclaim premium, New pension scheme, House rent allowance exemption, Educational loan, Life insurance and many more.

Investment declaration are the rights of every citizen of our country and we at Apoyo ensure that we will provide with the best possible service so that our clients are fully satisfied.
