Accounts Receivable

Accounts receivable are the lifeblood of a business's cash flow. Sometimes referred to as A/R, "accounts receivable" is the accounting term used to refer to the money that the business should receive from its customers for the goods or services it provided. Your business's accounts receivable are an important part of calculating your profitability, and provide the clearest indicator of the business's income.
