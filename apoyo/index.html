• Negotiation on transaction documents.

• Setting up e-commerce business platform, preparing structures for B2B and B2C business model (with FDI Investment), preparing documentation in this respect.

• Liaison for approvals from various regulators such as Reserve Bank of India, Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), where the transactions required such approvals.

• Drafting various EPC contracts such as supply contract, erection contract, works contract.

• Real estate advisory work, residential and commercial lease and leave and license agreement and advice on stamp duty and registration of the aforesaid documents.

• Title search, due diligence, vetting and negotiating property documents such as agreement to sale, lease deed.

Through these associations, she has serviced a wide range of clientele including companies in the infrastructure sector, power sector, real estate companies, investment companies, IT companies and foreign investors from various jurisdictions.




Mr. Ravindra Avhad

Legal Expert.

Ravindra completed his Law from Government law college- Mumbai in the year 2010 and left for South Hampton university for completing his LL.M in International Trade and Maritime law.

After completion of his LL.M in Maritime law from University of Southampton he joined chamber of Senior Counsel Mr. Prashant Pratap and gained versatile experience in the field of International Trade and Maritime law.

He has represented many International companies in shipping matters in Mumbai High Court.

He was associated with M/s. Seastar Ship Management Private Limited, MRK Law Office, PSP Law Office.

At PSP Law Office and MRK Law Office, he acquired diverse experience in handling claims, advising clients, relating to admiralty, cargo and protection and indemnity clubs and the following:

• Handling of disputes involving interpretation of Charterparty clauses and Bill of Lading clauses.

• Independent handling of cargo damage claims arising under Bill of Lading and Charterparty.

• Handling claims arising under multiple Marine Insurance policies in Consumer Forums.

• Handling mattersrelated to Customs duty.

• Determining liability of owners and protection and indemnity club in a Maritime claim/incident.

• To work on claims arising under Bill of Lading and carriage of goods by sea.

• Handling compensation claims by the crew members and determining the protection and indemnity clubs’ liability.

• Demurrage and despatch claims under Charterparty.

• Determining criminal and civil liability of crew owners and protection and indemnity clubs in different scenarios viz. death of stevedores during cargo and mooring operations.

• Securing bail for crew involved in accident onboard vessel.

• Visiting onboard vessel to investigate facts, physical verification, interview and take statements of crew members involved in a maritime incident.

• Arrest of vessels in lieu of maritime claims arising under the Arrest Convention 1952 and 1999 respectively.

• Filing caveats against release of sales proceeds in High Court of Mumbai to secure the claim of seafarers against the vessel auctioned in lieu of other claims.

• Vetting of charter parties agreements.

• Issuance/reply to legal notices.

He has also gained versatile experience in handling DRT, NCLT, consumer matters, 138 matters, summary suits and civil litigation.

He has diverse litigation experience at Bombay High Court. He is associated with retired chief Justice of Kolkata High Court Hon’ble Justice J. N. Patel and Maharashtra Police Department for wide range of consultancy and services.

He also has knowledge and experience in dealing with real estate properties, infrastructure and corporate & commercial laws.

He utilizes his expertise and commercial knowledge to achieve the optimum solutions for his clients and assists clients to resolve disputes arising from significant technology contracts.

Through these associations, he has serviced a wide range of clientele including companies in the maritime sector and other sectors from various jurisdictions.
